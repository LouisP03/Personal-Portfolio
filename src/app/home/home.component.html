Biography

I am currently a Final Year Computer Science student working towards a Bachelor’s Degree at the University of Birmingham, UK. As a student of a subject which I also consider one of my primary passions, I have a thirst for exploring and learning about all developments in technology, and where it intersects with daily life.

My passion for computers has led to a deep interest in programming, which has matured over the course of my studies in school and at university. The love I have for the field finds its roots in simply playing and experimenting, starting at a very young age, learning things by having a profound curiosity about how they work.

In addition to these particular interests, I also devote myself to a wide range of things about which I am enthusiastic, all the way from music to mechanics.

I have learnt, having been afforded many opportunities over the course of my studies, that collaboration and teamwork is something I enjoy tremendously; knowing how to strike a balance between being receptive of new ideas and approaches, and having the confidence to lead and influence other people. I particularly thrive in groups where backgrounds are very diverse, and I find that the learning of others’ experiences, be it academically, professionally, or personally, is quite refreshing.

To summarise my professional goals, it is my aim to become a Software Engineer, and to work in an environment where I know my genuine passion for the subject, and curiosity to delve into new concepts, can truly be reflected in the work I produce.
